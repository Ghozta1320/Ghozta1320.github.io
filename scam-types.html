Common Types of Scams

Scam Type Description Warning Signs How to Protect Yourself
Phone Scams Fraudulent calls claiming to be from government agencies or tech support Urgent requests, threats, requests for personal information Never give personal information over phone, verify caller's identity
Email Phishing Fake emails attempting to steal personal information Poor grammar, suspicious links, urgent demands Don't click unknown links, verify sender's address
Gift Card Scams Requests to pay with gift cards for services or debts Payment requested in gift cards, urgent pressure Never pay bills with gift cards, it's always a scam
