Scam Type Description Risk Level Common Methods
AI Trading Bot Scams Fake AI-powered trading systems promising high returns High Websites, social media
DeFi Rug Pulls Fraudulent decentralized finance projects High DeFi platforms
Recovery Scams False promises to recover lost crypto High Email, direct messages

Warning Signs

  • Urgent requests for immediate action
  • Demands for payment in cryptocurrency or gift cards
  • Unsolicited investment opportunities
  • Requests for personal or financial information
  • Promises of unrealistic returns or benefits
  • Pressure to keep communication secret
  • Poor grammar or spelling in official communications
  • Requests to download unknown software or apps

Protection Tips

  • Verify caller identity through official channels
  • Never share personal information without verification
  • Use multi-factor authentication for all accounts
  • Keep software and devices updated
  • Research before making any financial decisions
  • Use our scam detection tool for verification
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Consult with trusted family members or advisors
